  • 24/7 support within our resolution center

How to get started

  1. 1

    Sign up to become a host

    We want to ensure the best possible Host and Renter experience. To do so, all hosts are required to schedule a convenient time for a Roo representative to review the space and answer any additional questions.

  2. 2

    Connect with your renter

    You’ll receive a reservation request from your renter. Simply confirm the request and from there establish a convenient drop-off and pick-up time. Hosts must be present for both.

  3. 3

    Sit back, relax and get paid

    You never have to deal directly with money. Your payment is automatically sent at the end of each month. Enjoy reaping the benefits of this additional revenue stream.
