BECOME A VERIFIED
ROO HOST
Whether you're looking to save for that next vacation or to donate more to your favorite local charity, with Roo, you can passively earn to fund a number of different lifestyles. Sign-up to become a Roo host and let your space do the work.
Sign Up
1
Set an Appointment
Once you have signed up, you will receive an email requesting a convenient time and date for a Roo representative to come out and verify your space(s). This is a great opportunity for you to get a better feel for how the hosting process will work.
2
Brief Assessment
The representative collects general information and verifies your licensed photo ID. From there, the Roo Standards Guide will be used to evaluate specifications, accessibility, cleanliness, and other potential features of the space. We'll input all information on to RooStorage.com - you won’t have to worry about it.
3
Background Check
After completing the initial assessment of your space, you'll receive an invite to complete a background check byCheckr, the company trusted by giants like Oracle and IntuitTurboTax. This check gives peace of mind to your Renters and adds another layer of protection on top of the provided insurance.
4
Payment Information
Last but not least, you will need to input your preferred method of payment into StripeConnect, used by virtually every online business. Transactions are automated so you don't have to worry about any cash hand-offs. Just sit back, relax, and get paid!
